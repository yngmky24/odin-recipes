Smooth Sweet Tea

Description

Sweet tea is a classic drink, and this recipe is a breeze to make with your favourite tea. Sweeten the tea while it's hot, then keep it chilled in the fridge for th perfect cold refreshment to serve over ice on hot summer days!

Ingredients

  • 1 pinch baking soda
  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 6 tea bags
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 6 cups cool water
