Smooth Sweet Tea

Description

This lemon tea is my favourite tea. It is a sweet and sour tea – you taste the honey, then the lemon. Great for soothing sore throats. Yum, yum, yum!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 2 teaspoons honey
  • 1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar, or to taste
